🏥 Support Services Access — Q4 2025
Medical Care Access
Shows the percentage of Q4 SGBV survivors who received medical care. Low access indicates barriers to health services for survivors—a critical protection gap.
Psychosocial Support
Percentage of SGBV survivors who received psychosocial or mental health support. Tracks access to trauma-informed care—essential for recovery.
Report to Authority
Percentage of SGBV survivors who reported the incident to authorities. Low rates reflect barriers to justice (stigma, fear, lack of trust) and underreporting of sexual violence.
Perpetrators Arrested
Percentage of SGBV cases where the perpetrator was arrested. Key accountability indicator—low rates suggest impunity for sexual violence.
Resulting Pregnancies
Percentage of SGBV cases that resulted in pregnancy. A critical protection indicator—pregnancy from rape requires specialised support and highlights need for access to reproductive health services.
